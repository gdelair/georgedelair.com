MonoDraft - Minimal, distraction-free browser writing

January 13, 2026 2 min read

Last night I needed a thing and nothing was simple enough...

A couple hours later... I had built the thing.

Meet MonoDraft

It's really at home on a Laptop or an iPad with a keyboard but I tried to make it as mobile friendly as possible as well.

No ads... no bloat... no complex systems...

Just simple, rich text editing with markdown or HTML export.

There is no syncing, only local storage in the browser for now.

I'm exploring cloud options for document saving but it does what it says on the tin so I'm happy to call it done for my needs. I do have a few small things on the "roadmap" but it's minimal.

Also, on-demand software is probably the future... Just saying
