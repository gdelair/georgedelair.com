Privacy Policy for PomBar

Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

PomBar is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe productivity tools should help you focus, not track you.

Data Collection

PomBar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All information stays on your device:

  • Timer settings (work duration, break duration, sessions per cycle) are stored locally on your Mac using system preferences.
  • Statistics (completed pomodoros, streaks) are stored locally on your Mac and never leave your device.
  • Preferences (sound settings, menu bar display options) are stored locally on your Mac.

Network Access

PomBar does not connect to the internet. The app functions entirely offline and makes no network requests.

Third-Party Services

PomBar does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data.

Data Storage

All app data is stored in your Mac's standard application preferences directory. You can reset all data by removing the app and its preferences from your system.

Children's Privacy

PomBar does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version with an updated date.
